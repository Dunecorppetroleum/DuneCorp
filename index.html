DuneCorp Petroleum CC 

DuneCorp Petroleum CC is a Namibian Registered Petroleum Export / Import , Domestic and Marine Supply Company based in Walvisbay with a nework across the SADC region. DuneCorp Petroleum prides itself with an Outstanding Customer service track record and Compliance to all Statutory Regulations with regards to the handling and transportation of Petroleum products.

Product and Destination Mediums

Through our network of supplies DuneCorp Petroleum has pick-up points across Namibia namely Walvisbay , Otjiwarongo , Windhoek and Walvisbay. Regionally we supply to the following countries Botswana, Zambia, Zimbabwe and DRC. Our merchandise and products can be delivered by Road. With a fleet of solid trucks at our disposal DuneCorp Petroleum has the capacity to reach any destination respectively in the SADC area.
